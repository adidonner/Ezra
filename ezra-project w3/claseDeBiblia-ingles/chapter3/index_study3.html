God Made People

THESE ARE THE PEOPLE God made. Can you see them in the picture? They are behind the flowers in the middle of the picture. Can you point to them? The man's name is Adam. The lady's name is Eve. Adam and Eve did not have a mother and father. God made them out of dust from the ground, and then He made them become alive. God made them happy and good. They love God and God loves them.

In the picture you see them looking up toward God. No wonder they are so happy.
Genesis 2:7-9

Questions
